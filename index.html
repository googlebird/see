You may see this message on any screen across CoreAccess
Your account does not have the privileges to access this person. This is a security feature.
There are 4 possibilities.
(a) They have been set to inactive. Check the "Include Inactive" on the Employee maintenance screen is checked, Then try and find them in the list of people.
(b) You do not have permission to see the department they are in. You can rectify this by checking Maintenance > User Setup > Bring up your username > Hit departments Ensure you have all departments checked.
(c) You do not have access to the zone that these people are in Maintenance > User Setup > Bring up your username Check that you have Corporation Access, or access to the persons zone.
Corporation Access will be denoted by a value in the Corporation Column.
(d) The department that they are a member of has been deleted from the system, In this case you will need to open a ticket at http://service.tds.ie
